*Ridge Racer* isn't unlike most other arcade-to-console franchises in how simple its games are, but *Type 4* really shines in its deeply diligent and laser-focused pursuit of delivering a real urban racing experience. there's no single element here that is out of place nor unproductive to that pursuit<b>:</b> not the UI, not the cars, not the controls, not the models, environments, or backgrounds, and *definitely* not the soundtrack. the atmosphere *Type 4* builds for itself is ostensibly a labor of love, and one that really is pure magic to experience.

that magic holds you, not letting go until the finish line is crossed and then some. enraptured by feel and flight, you play race after race, perfecting the turns and takes and turns again. as you catch your bottom lip between teeth in the tension of a sketchy powerslide for first place, backed by the most passionate club breakbeat you've ever heard, you just can't help but think that yeah, these are the kinds of games we invented computers to play.

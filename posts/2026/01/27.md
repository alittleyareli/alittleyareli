###### note: this post is a [mirror](https://bckl.gg/qZag) of my Backloggd review.

this game is almost entirely unremarkable, which isn't a surprising sentiment, sure, but i do mean that in the most literal sense. there really isn't much going on here to remark on. it's a 3v3 arena style hero-shooter, a decade too late to the punch, so creatively bankrupt in visual and aesthetic presentation the most i could muster in critique was "ok, what a snoozefest" before uninstalling. Highguard is definitely undeserving of the rancid hate campaign that has tided over its release, but that's about the best i can say for this game.

if nothing else, i do sincerely appreciate the revelation that Geoff Keighley is also deeply in love with a game that is, by all accounts, two stars at best. just like the rest of us.

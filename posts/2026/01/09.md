just as the protagonist claims to love "*the pyramidal structure of verbal constructions*," so too, does this game derive its satisfaction from re-shaping your agency as the reader of a visual novel into a short, intriguing exercise in perverse voyeurism. you are The Player, and therefore omniscient, but not omnipotent. you are trapped, just as well as that girl is, or, as the title would suggest<b>:</b> a bag of milk, inside a bag of milk. a thoughtful game from start to finish, and one that hits harder depending how closely your lived experience matches that of the protagonist's.
